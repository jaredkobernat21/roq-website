Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026 (supersedes the June 24, 2026 version)

This Privacy Policy describes how ROQ Innovation LLC (“ROQ”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ROQ mobile application (“App”). By using ROQ, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Home Profile: Your home’s name, address, photo, purchase price, and other details you choose to add.

Projects: Renovation projects, maintenance tasks, and administrative items you log, including descriptions, costs, dates, and categories.

Experiences: Memories, rituals, and moments you capture, including photos, descriptions, and dates.

Documents: Files you upload to the Vault, including mortgage documents, insurance policies, warranties, and maintenance records.

Account Information: Your name and email address used to create your account.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including features used, frequency of use, and session duration.

Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version.

Pulse History: Your home’s health score history over time, derived from your project and experience activity.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

If you connect third-party services (such as property value data providers), we may receive information from those services as described at the time of connection.

1.4 Subscription and Billing Information

ROQ subscriptions are purchased and billed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment card information. We receive limited transaction information from Apple or Google — such as your subscription plan, trial status, purchase date, and renewal status — which we use to manage your access to the App.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalize the ROQ experience, including your PULSE score, STUDIO, EVOLUTION timeline, FINGERPRINT, and PORTFOLIO.
  • Power SLADE, our AI home advisor, which uses your home data, Vault documents, project history, experience history, and conversation history to provide personalized guidance.
  • Send notifications including project reminders, experience reminders, overdue alerts, weekly home reports, and pulse state changes.
  • Manage your subscription, trial period, and access to paid features.
  • Analyze and improve the App’s features and performance.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
3

SLADE AI Advisor

SLADE is ROQ’s AI home advisor, powered by the Claude API (provided by Anthropic, PBC). When you interact with SLADE, your messages and relevant home context — including Vault documents, project data, experience data, and conversation history — are sent to Anthropic’s API to generate responses.

Anthropic’s data handling practices are governed by their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy. We do not sell your data to Anthropic or any third party. Data shared with SLADE is used solely to provide you with AI-powered home guidance.

To maintain service quality and protect against abuse, SLADE usage is subject to reasonable, generously-set usage limits. See Section 6 of our Terms of Service for details.

4

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your ROQ account.

5

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers (such as Supabase, Anthropic, Apple, and Google) who assist us in operating the App and processing subscriptions, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice provided to you.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent.
6

Your Choices and Rights

You may access, update, or delete your home data and account at any time through the App’s settings. You may also:

  • Delete individual projects, experiences, or Vault documents at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@roqhome.com.
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications in the App’s settings.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.

If you are located in California or the European Economic Area, you may have additional rights under applicable law, including the right to access, correct, or port your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7

Children’s Privacy

ROQ is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of ROQ after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
